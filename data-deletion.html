How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data from GK Ocean in the following ways:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the GK Ocean app
  2. Go to Settings/Profile
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Method 2: Email Request

You can also request data deletion by:

  1. Sending an email to [Your Email]
  2. Include your account email in the request
  3. Use subject line "Data Deletion Request - GK Ocean"

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once completed.
